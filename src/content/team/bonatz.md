Hedda is a PhD researcher in the Coastal Risk and Sea Level Rise working group
at the University of Kiel. Her work focuses on how spatial patterns of
population development influence exposure and vulnerability to coastal hazards,
including processes of flood-induced mobility and urbanization. She has a
background in Geography and was part of the CoCliCo project, where she developed
spatial population projections under different socio-economic scenarios using an
extended gravity model.
