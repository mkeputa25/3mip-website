Dr. Muhammad Abdur Rahaman serves as Director of the Center for People & Environ
(CPE). He has worked in the climate science and policy field for more than 20
years, serving national and international organizations as a researcher,
practitioner, and development actor across climate change, climate displacement
and migration solutions, disaster risk reduction, climate-smart agricultural
innovations, and the climate-and-health nexus.
