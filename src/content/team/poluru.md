Ramesh Poluru did his Masters and PhD in Population Studies from Sri
Venkateswara University, Tirupati, India. Dr Poluru has more than two decades
of experience in the conceptualization, implementation, and monitoring of
large-scale socioeconomic, demographic, behavioural, biological, and operations
research studies, and in the analysis and interpretation of primary and
secondary data using both qualitative and quantitative approaches. He has
published about 40 research papers in peer-reviewed journals, serves on
research-proposal evaluation advisory committees, reviews for over forty
journals and various international granting agencies, and sits on the editorial
boards of eight international journals.
