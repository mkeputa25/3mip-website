### Logo (added Phase 4D)

An official 3MIP logo exists — `final-3mip-logo.png` on the current Weebly
site. The new site uses a typographic wordmark (the design-system decision
from Phase 1). Bell decides one of:

1. **Adopt the new wordmark site-wide** (current default — it ships unless
   overridden);
2. **Keep the existing logo** — requires exporting a clean SVG/PNG from the
   Weebly asset and placing it in the Header, Footer, favicon, and OG image;
3. **Commission a refresh** — out of v1 budget scope unless Bell funds it.

Until decided, the wordmark ships. **Do not mix both** — a site that shows
the wordmark in the header and the old logo in the OG image reads as
unfinished.
